Task 2 ‚Äì AI Bias Detector :

3 Signs of Bias in AI Content:
1. Stereotyping: The AI repeats harmful or unfair assumptions about a group (e.g., gender, race, nationality).
2. Imbalanced Representation: One group is shown positively while another is shown negatively without reason.
3. Exclusion or Erasure: Important groups or voices are missing when they should be included.

2 Tips to Avoid AI Bias:
1. Use Diverse Training Data: Ensure the AI training data includes many cultures and viewpoints to keep it fair and balanced.
2. Human Review and Feedback: Always have a human review AI content, especially for sensitive topics, to catch and correct bias.
